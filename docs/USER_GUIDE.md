# Resident user guide

## Submit an application

1. Open `/apply`.
2. Enter full name, 11-digit Qatar ID, email, Qatar contact number, and villa/address.
3. Select one or more active facilities.
4. For monthly facilities, choose a duration within the configured minimum and maximum.
5. Review each line price and the total in QAR.
6. Upload the Qatar ID and payment proof as JPG, PNG, WebP, or PDF.
7. Submit and retain the displayed application reference.

Each upload must be 5 MB or smaller. Images may be compressed before storage.

## Pricing

- **Monthly:** monthly price multiplied by selected months.
- **Per booking:** booking price with no month selector.
- **Free:** no payment amount.

The submitted price is saved with the application. Later facility-price changes do not alter an existing application.

## Renewal

Use the same email and Qatar ID as the approved resident record. A renewal is sent for manager review while the existing QR pass remains available until its current access expires.

## After submission

The confirmation page shows the reference, status, and calculated total. The manager verifies the identity document, selected facilities, and payment proof. An approval or rejection email is then created.

## QR pass

After approval, the email contains a QR image and pass link. Present the QR pass at the authorised gate scanner. Do not share it with another person.

## Privacy

Qatar ID and payment images are sensitive. Use a private device, verify the site address, and avoid sending documents through unrelated channels.
