Key Features

  • Online resident applications
  • Payment proof verification
  • QR pass generation after approval
  • Facility and membership management
  • Real-time attendance logs
  • Reports and export tools

How it works

  1. ApplyResident selects facilities, enters details, pays, and uploads documents.
  2. ReviewAdmin checks QID, payment proof, and requested access.
  3. ApproveSystem creates a QR pass only after admin approval.
  4. ScanSecurity scans the QR pass at the facility gate.
